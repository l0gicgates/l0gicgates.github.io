Condition Assessments

Condition assessments are often a vital step in the process of building acquisitions, repurposing, modifications and expansions. This service provides our client with a thorough evaluation of the property, outlining the current condition of the structure and identifying areas where repairs are warranted.

Peer Reviews

Peer Reviews serve as a valuable checks-and-balances assurance offered to the client that are a benefit to both Owner and Engineer of Record. This service entails a complete evaluation of the construction documents to ensure the building structure is adequate and meets all pertinent requirements of the governing building codes. Items resolved through the peer review process often result in savings to the project in both time and cost by eliminating otherwise unforeseen design errors that later arise during construction.

Value Engineering

SM+S takes the peer review process a step further by offering a complete Value Engineering service. Utilizing our knowledge earned from 30+ years of combined experience in the industry, SM+S will identify areas in the construction documents where there reductions in the project construction cost can be realized. This is achieved in a variety of ways from optimizing engineering designs, alternate framing solutions that save in construction materials and scheduling, etc.

Feasibility Studies

Feasibility Study services are performed early on in a project’s lifespan. This service is provided to assist our clients in determining whether or not a project or portion(s) of a project are financially and practically feasible. Before the project is advanced into design, a feasibility study will provide valuable insight in deciding if the project vision is worth pursuing or if an alternate approach will be better suited for the clients need.
