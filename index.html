SERVICES OFFERED

Structural Design

SM+S offers a full suite of Structural Engineering Design Services including New Building Design, Structural Retrofitting, Building Expansions, and Tenant Improvements in both the Commercial, Industrial and Residential sectors.

Peer Reviews

Peer Reviews serve as valuable checks-and-balances, benefiting both the Owner and Engineer of Record. Our service entails a complete evaluation of the construction documents and structural design to ensure that both meet the desired design criteria and requirements of the governing building codes. Items resolved through the peer review process often result in project savings, both time and cost, by eliminating design issues that would otherwise not be discovered until the plan check process or construction.

Feasibility Studies

Feasibility Study services are performed early on in a project’s design process. This service provides the information our clients require to determine whether or not a project, or portion(s) of a project, are financially and practically feasible. Before the project is advanced into design, our feasibility study will provide valuable insight in deciding if the project vision is worth pursuing, or if an alternate approach or system would be better suited for the clients' needs.

Value Engineering

SM+S takes the peer review process a step further by offering a complete Value Engineering service. Utilizing our knowledge acquired from 30+ years of combined experience in the industry, SM+S will identify areas in the construction documents where project savings can be realized. This is achieved in a variety of ways including optimization of engineering designs and alternate framing solutions that save in construction materials and scheduling

Condition Assessments

Condition assessments are often a vital step in the process of acquiring, repurposing, modifying, or expanding an existing structure. We provide our clients with a thorough structural evaluation of the property; documenting the current condition of the structure and identifying areas where repairs or enhancements are warranted.

Structural Retrofits and Repairs

Whether your needs include General Building Repairs, Structural Evaluations per ASCE 41, Seismic Strengthening, Wind Mitigation Design, Seismic Risk Assessments with PMLs or Targeted Scope Retrofits, we provide valuable solutions for all structural retrofitting and repair needs.
